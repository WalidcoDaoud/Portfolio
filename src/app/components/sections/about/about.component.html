My Story

I started studying Computer Engineering in 2021, drawn to its role as the future of innovation. Though fascinated by both hardware and software, I chose programming for its creative problem-solving potential. Now, I'm focused on building impactful solutions through code while continuously expanding my skills.

I began my career as a Technical Support Intern in a consulting company, handling L1 tasks like user management, access control, and direct client troubleshooting. This experience sharpened my problem-solving skills and my communication skills, before I transitioned into frontend development, where I now combine technical expertise with a user-focused mindset.

Currently I am working as a Fullstack Developer at a consulting company, I work on diverse projects using Angular, React, Material UI, and Angular Material for frontend development. On the backend, I primarily work with .NET, along with SQL Server and MongoDB for database solutions. This role allows me to combine my passion for clean, responsive UIs with robust, scalable backend systems. Every project is a chance to improve. I'm constantly learning, experimenting, and refining my skills to write cleaner code, design more efficient systems, and grow as a developer.
